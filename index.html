Did You Know

Oil Rich

Oron is the most producing oil community in Nigeria. They produce 80% of Akwa-ibom crude oil.

Food Hub

Oron is the cure for delicious sea foods ( crabs, fresh fishes, prawns, oysters, crayfish and so on...) A place where people from other part of Nigeria come to buy crayfish, afang, fish etc.

Attraction & culture

Oron is home to one of the oldest museum and high school in Nigeria, Methodist boys high school, Oron. Founded in 1905

Resource rich

Oron is home to a lot of natural resources including silica sand, salt,Limestone,Uranium, Manganese, clay.

President Oron union - Dr. (Mrs.) Janet Asuquo Amba

Wife of the Commissioner of Police, Bayelsa State, Mr. Asuquo Amba. Until her emergence, she was the Vice President-General 1 of the union. Mrs. Amba took over the reins of office from Dr. Effiong Edunam, a one-time Akwa Ibom Commissioner for Education and a university don. .
